# Notice of Non-Consent for Broker Profiling

## Formal Notice

This document provides express notice that the repository owner does **not** consent to the use of this repository, or any of its contents, for data-broker profiling, people-search indexing, commercial aggregation, inferential scoring, dossier creation, identity enrichment, or derivative commercial person-based products.

Any broker, reseller, profiling company, commercial AI system operator, identity-resolution vendor, or similar entity is on notice that this repository is **not** offered as raw material for commercial profiling or exploitation.

## No Consent Granted

No consent is granted for any of the following activities:

- harvesting or scraping repository content for broker use;
- incorporating repository content into people-search or subject-profile databases;
- enriching, updating, or validating broker-held records using repository content;
- generating risk scores, trust scores, identity scores, reputation scores, or similar inferential outputs tied to the subject of the record;
- creating dossiers, summaries, background-style reports, or derivative identity products for sale, subscription, licensing, or internal commercial use;
- republishing repository content in commercial directories, lookup tools, search portals, profile pages, or resale products;
- correlating repository content with external databases in order to expand or monetize a subject profile;
- using repository content in machine-learning, ranking, scoring, lead-generation, or commercial identity-resolution systems directed at person-based monetization.

## Public Does Not Mean Consensual

The fact that repository content may be publicly accessible does **not** mean that the repository owner consents to commercial profiling, extraction, repackaging, or monetization.

Public visibility is not an invitation to:

- profile;
- package;
- score;
- resell;
- enrich;
- correlate;
- rank;
- commercialize;
- or distort the record through derivative broker systems.

No broker or commercial actor may reasonably interpret public accessibility as a grant of consent for exploitative reuse.

## Objection to Profiling and Enrichment

The repository owner expressly objects to all non-consensual broker practices that convert curated public-facing materials into commercial identity assets, including:

- profile expansion;
- data enrichment;
- identity stitching;
- inferential reconstruction;
- commercial person-matching;
- background-style aggregation;
- commercial AI synthesis directed toward subject monetization;
- downstream republication that strips context, provenance, chronology, or authorship.

These practices are rejected in full unless separately authorized in writing.

## Objection to Derivative Identity Products

The repository owner does not consent to the creation of derivative identity products based in whole or in part on repository content.

This includes, without limitation:

- subject profiles;
- people-search pages;
- data broker entries;
- “about” summaries generated for monetized platforms;
- rank-ordered or scored identity outputs;
- blended or stitched records combining repository content with third-party sources;
- commercial research products or intelligence files based on the subject of the record.

Any such derivative use is contrary to the stated position of this repository.

## Notice to Commercial AI and Automated Systems

No consent is granted for automated systems, including commercial AI or machine-learning systems, to use repository content for:

- subject-based commercial profiling;
- monetized identity inference;
- dossier generation;
- people-search enhancement;
- broker enrichment workflows;
- or person-centered ranking, screening, or classification products.

Automated access does not excuse unauthorized use. Machine readability does not create permission.

## Preservation of Record Integrity

This repository is curated to preserve accurate authorship, provenance, chronology, and interpretive integrity. Broker extraction and derivative profiling routinely strip context, introduce stale or partial data, and generate misleading composite narratives.

The repository owner objects to such uses and rejects any claim that downstream broker representations faithfully reflect the original record.

## Reservation of Rights and Position

This notice is intended to make the repository owner’s position unmistakable.

The repository owner reserves all rights, objections, and available positions relating to unauthorized commercial profiling, extraction, republication, identity enrichment, and derivative person-based exploitation.

Nothing in this repository shall be interpreted as:

- consent to profiling;
- consent to enrichment;
- consent to resale;
- consent to dossier creation;
- consent to derivative commercial identity products;
- or waiver of any objection to such practices.

## Instruction to Brokers and Similar Entities

Any commercial actor wishing to use repository content beyond ordinary non-commercial public review must cease unauthorized use and seek express written authorization before proceeding.

Until such authorization is granted, the answer is no.

## Final Notice

This repository is a curated public record and academic archive, not a consent engine for broker profiling.

Any broker, aggregator, or commercial profiling entity that touches this repository for subject-based monetization does so against expressed non-consent and with full notice of objection.
