# Broker Use Policy

## Statement of Policy

This repository is a curated academic, identity-verification, and public-record integrity archive. It is made available for lawful public viewing, reference, and review. It is **not** published as a free commercial intake source for data brokers, people-search companies, profile aggregators, commercial AI dossier builders, identity-resolution vendors, or derivative reputation systems.

No broker, aggregator, profiling company, data reseller, or similar commercial actor is authorized to treat this repository as an unrestricted source for extraction, ingestion, enrichment, republication, or monetization.

## Prohibited Broker Activities

Absent prior written authorization, the following activities are prohibited as unauthorized broker use of this repository and its contents:

- scraping, harvesting, or systematically extracting repository content;
- ingesting repository content into broker, aggregator, or people-search databases;
- combining repository content with third-party datasets for commercial profiling or identity enrichment;
- creating dossiers, background-style profiles, risk scores, ranking systems, or inferential identity products;
- republishing repository content in commercial directories, search portals, people-finder services, or resale products;
- transforming repository content into derivative commercial records, lead-generation products, compliance products, or marketing intelligence assets;
- using repository content to enhance, validate, update, or cross-link broker-held files about the subject of the record;
- licensing, sublicensing, selling, or redistributing extracted repository content or derivative data products built from it;
- using machine-readable access, indexing, or public accessibility as a pretext for commercial reuse.

## No Implied License

No implied license is granted for broker access, reuse, or monetization.

The public availability of repository content does not create consent, waiver, acquiescence, abandonment, or unrestricted commercial reuse rights. Visibility is not permission. Accessibility is not authorization. Indexability is not licensure.

Any broker or commercial actor claiming otherwise is interpreting public availability beyond its lawful and intended scope.

## Restricted Commercial Uses

The following classes of use are expressly restricted unless separately authorized in writing:

- commercial identity resolution;
- people-search indexing;
- commercial profile enrichment;
- dossier creation;
- broker republication;
- resale or relicensing;
- inferential analytics tied to the subject of the record;
- commercial AI synthesis for profiling, scoring, or person-based commercialization;
- use in products that generate revenue from the collection, packaging, ranking, or resale of personal or professional information.

## Preservation of Record Integrity

This repository is curated to preserve record integrity, provenance, authorship, and interpretive accuracy. Broker extraction and derivative republication create a substantial risk of distortion, decontextualization, mischaracterization, stale replication, and unfair identity framing.

For that reason, no commercial actor may lawfully assume that copying or repackaging repository content preserves its meaning, authority, chronology, or evidentiary context.

Any downstream broker representation based on repository extraction is the responsibility of the extracting party and may be treated as unauthorized derivative use, miscontextualization, or exploitative commercial repackaging.

## Compensation and Licensing Requirement

Any broker, data aggregator, reseller, identity vendor, or similar commercial actor seeking to use repository content for a commercial or quasi-commercial purpose must first:

1. obtain express written authorization; and
2. enter into a separate compensation or licensing arrangement.

There is no automatic right to commercial reuse.

Commercial value extracted from this repository is not free inventory for brokers. If a third party intends to derive economic benefit from this repository or its contents, that use must be licensed and compensated in advance.

## Non-Consent to Profiling and Enrichment

The repository owner does not consent to:

- broker profiling;
- identity enrichment;
- inferential ranking;
- dossier creation;
- resale-oriented aggregation;
- commercial correlation with external broker data;
- automated commercial synthesis designed to expand a subject profile for downstream monetization.

Any such activity undertaken without written authorization is contrary to the stated policy of this repository.

## Evidence of Notice

This policy is published to provide clear advance notice of the repository’s position regarding broker access and commercial reuse. Any broker, aggregator, or profiling entity that accesses this repository after publication of this policy does so with notice that commercial exploitation is restricted and not impliedly authorized.

Continued access for commercial extraction after notice may be documented as evidence of intentional disregard of the repository’s stated access and reuse conditions.

## No Limitation on Other Rights

This policy does not waive, limit, or surrender any other rights, claims, objections, remedies, or positions that may exist under applicable law, platform policy, copyright principles, unfair competition principles, misappropriation theories, privacy-related doctrines, contractual terms, or future enforcement frameworks.

All rights not expressly granted are reserved.

This repository is public for review, not public for commercial capture.

Data brokers and similar entities are on notice: if they want commercial use, they must obtain permission, negotiate compensation, and stop pretending that “public” means “free for exploitation.”
